About

I have had the opportunity to participate in service activities in three countries. I have built a school in Malawi, taught refugees in Jordan, and performed daily service in Honduras. Each of these expereinces has helped me gain a greater understanding of this world and a deeper appreciation for all that I have. I hope you enjoy a brief overview of each experience and how it has impacted me!
